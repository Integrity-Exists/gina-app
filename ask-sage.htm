Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I file a Motion? — Start by identifying the type of motion you need. Prepare a document stating your request, include supporting facts, and file it with the court clerk.
  • How should I address the judge? — Always address the judge as \"Your Honor.\" Speak respectfully and clearly.
  • What if I can’t afford filing fees? — You may file a Fee Waiver Request or "In Forma Pauperis" form asking the court to waive fees due to hardship.
